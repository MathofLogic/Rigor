The Rigor Suite is a stipulated instrument built on the Propagation Logic frame. Its findings are disclosures to be checked, not verdicts to be trusted; every audit ends with its own confidence note. A paper resting on a hidden commitment may be entirely correct — the suite reports what was undisclosed, never that it is false.
